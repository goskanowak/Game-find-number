How to play

The computer will draw an integer from the pool of numbers you enter in the text field. Your task is to guess the number. You try for as long as you guess, and the computer always tells you whether your number is bigger or smaller than what you are looking for. Good luck :)

x

Hint

Is it possible to act smarter than guessing in turn 1, 2, 3, 4, ...? Let's take a case of 16 numbers. If the computer gives you information if the number proposed by you is bigger, smaller or equal to the one chosen by the computer, you can start with 8. If the number chosen by the computer is less than 8, you can eliminate all numbers from 8 to 16 in advance. If the number chosen the computer is larger than 8, you can eliminate all numbers from 1 to 8. In both cases, you can eliminate half the possibilities. In the next step you will eliminate half of the remaining numbers. And so on, eliminating half the remaining numbers each time. This division in half is called binary search and regardless of the number from 1 to 16 you choose the computer with this method, you should guess the correct answer in no more than 4 attempts.
